Medicine Service

The Addiction to Online Medicine Service (AtOM) is a free and confidential service which specifically focuses on the harm caused by medication or illicit drugs bought on the internet. We offer help for people who live in the boroughs of Westminster, Kensington and Chelsea, and Hammersmith and Fulham, who are experiencing problems with these drugs.
