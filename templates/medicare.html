Healthcare with a single tap

You can get consultation in a matter of minutes from a registered doctor via video calling for physical illness, primary healthcare, maternal and elderly healthcare, or neonatal care and counselling.

Time and distance are no longer a matter of concern. Easily receive advice on physical illness or health issues through video calling.
  • 1
    Create Appointments

    Download the "MediCare" app from the Google Play Store and create your account in less than a minute, and make appointments easily.

  • 2
    Get Medical Consultation via Video Calling

    You can make an instant appointment and get the advice of a doctor in a video call, or you can make an appointment with a specialist doctor of your choice for the next time.

  • 3
    Get E-Prescription

    As soon as the video call ends, you will get the e-prescription given by the doctor in your app. You will also be given a reminder to take the medicine on time.
