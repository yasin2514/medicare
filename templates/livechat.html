Live Chat

Physical illness, primary healthcare, maternal and elderly healthcare, or neonatal care and counselling- whichever might be your need, you can connect with a registered doctor through video calling within minutes and get proper consultation.

Makes healthcare easier by connecting with a doctor via video call whenever you need it.
  • Download and Register

    Download the "MediCare" app from the Google Play Store and create your account in less than a minute.

  • Get Medical Consultation

    You can take instant consultation or you can schedule an appointment with a specialist doctor of your choice for the nex time.

  • Get E-prescription

    As soon as the video call ends, you will get the e-prescription given by the doctor in your app. You will also be given a reminder to take the medicine on time.
